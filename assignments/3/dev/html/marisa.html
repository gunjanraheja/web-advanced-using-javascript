Monitor Tracings, 2006-Present, Digital & Analog
Monitor Tracings(2006) portrays Marisa Olsonâ€™s definition of post internet art, to be inspired online and then create something offline. It is a series of Images of headphones, radios, telephones and printers. These Objects such as headphones or radios are searched on google and traced off the computer onto office paper using a mechanical pencil. Olson believes in creating art offline after being inspired online. The piece is mainly to address the importance and effects of internet on art and culture, which can be done on networks but can and should also exist offline.
