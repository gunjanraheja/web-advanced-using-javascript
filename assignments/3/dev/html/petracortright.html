PETRA CORTRIGHT
_
VVebcam, 2007, Web
Petra Cortright’s VVebcam(2007) portrays the effects and states of watching video online. It brings in the aspect of producing and consuming content. The artwork inserts a series of animations to the live video with background music. The main highlight is the passive surveillance of the video screen, reflected by the viewer’s experience instantly. She used a 20$ webcam and used default effects to record the work.
