MICHAEL BELL SMITH
_
Moving, Endless (2008), Digital piece
Moving Endless is a beautiful piece by Michael Bell Smith that explores visual culture through the use of digital technologies. This piece consists of five digital light boxes with five digital files. The visual elements are picked and positioned within an unexpected aesthetic conceptual framework.
