JAMES BRIDLE
_
Dronestagram, 2012, Website and App
Bridle created Dronestagram, a website which pressurizes the government for better transparency. The main objective is to build moral connections between foreign countries. The website has received online attention with thousands of followers. It displays images (satellite) of areas subjected to drone attacks building an ethical connection. It has been featured in many websites and magazines. It has also been awarded an Honorary Mention at the Prix Ars Electronica 2013. http://www.dronestagr.am/
