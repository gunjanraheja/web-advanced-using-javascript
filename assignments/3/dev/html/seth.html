SETH PRICE
_
Dispersion, 2002, Essay & Book
Price follows the mutability of the internet archive refusing to solidify previous projects. Price’s Dispersion(2002) investigates the significance of distribution – contemporary art and media. Concentrating on consumption and dispersion, he explores the redistribution of music and sound. He takes a positive view towards life and states: “Sometimes its good to go forward and then double back, and circle around again. To those who turned their feet around so their tracks would confuse their pursuers: why not walk backward?”
