CORY ARCANGEL
_
Fuck Negativity, 2015, Clothing line
Fuck Negativity is a piece by Cory Arcangel that explores inspiration and positivity. He designs a clothing line that is not only for the brand but for making a more positive attitude towards the society today. “Life is nothing but a never ending up-hill battle. It is important to keep your head up and moving forward. Gotta stay on it. Lets do this. FUCK NEGATIVITY”, is one of the most inspirational quotes by him. He released the new line on Cyber Monday.
